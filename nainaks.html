5 days The Great Rift Valley .

Enjoy our 5 Days tour of Nairobi - Lake Nakuru

From the eyecatching view of the Escarpment to Lake Nakuru National Park with scenic views of Mt.Longonot, to boat ride on Lake Naivasha that takes you to Crescent Island for a walking safari. and drive to the floor of the Riftvalley to Masai Mara Game drives and get the best opportunity of spotting the Big Five. boat ride on Lake Naivasha that takes you to Crescent Island for a walking safari. This package goes for $650 per person which includes the following

Day Locations Accomodation Meals
1 Nairobi - Lake Nakuru Rhino Camp. Lunch & Dinner
2 LAKE NAKURU/LAKE NAIVASHA. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
3 LAKE NAIVASHA/MAASAI MARA Taphe Guest House. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

Tour
Itinerary

Here's a scope of the experience you will get

  • 01 NAIROBI/LAKE NAKURU

    Depart Nairobi in the morning and drive to the floor of the Rift Valley with a brief stop at the Rift Valley viewpoint to take the most eyecatching view of the Escarpment. Proceed to Lake Nakuru National Park with scenic views of Mt.Longonot,LakeNaivasha and Lake Elementaitaen route, which are in the Great Rift from afar on the main highway to Nakuru. Game viewing en route To with picnic lunch followed by an afternoon game drive. Besides the Flamingos that the lake is famous for, there are approx 400 other species of birds visiting the lake. Also, many species of game can be found in the park including the introduced Rothschild's giraffe, black and white rhino, waterbuck, reedbuck, lion, buffalo, leopard, baboon and many species of plains game. Dinner and overnight at Rhino camp.

  • 02LAKE NAKURU/LAKE NAIVASHA

    After breakfast in your hotel depart Nakuru, for Naivasha arriving in time for lunch. Afternoon boat ride on Lake Naivasha that takes you to Crescent Island for a walking safari. This is one of the few places in the world where one can wander on foot amidst wild animals - there are no predators. Return to camp for dinner and overnight. Rhino camp.

  • 03LAKE NAIVASHA/MAASAI MARA

    Depart Naivasha after breakfast and drive further along the floor of the Rift Valley to Maasai Mara Game Reserve, arriving in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive as you get a feel of this vast game filled reserve. Dinner and overnight at Taphe Guest House.

  • 04 MAASAI MARA

    Spend the day in Maasai Mara, Kenya's most popular game sanctuary where you will have the best opportunity of spotting the Big Five. Game drives are flexible, with the option of going out with picnic lunch boxes to spend entire day in the park, or you can choose to have an early morning and late afternoon game drives. At extra cost you may visit a local Masai village to see their nomadic lifestyle; the Masai are famous for their bravery, as they have co-existed with the animals over the years. All meals and overnight at Taphe Guest House.

  • 05 MAASAI MARA to Nairobi

    Another morning game drive in Mara after breakfast, then drive back to Nairobi in the late afternoon. Drop off at your hotel.
