About Us

From the charm evoked in the life of the Masai Mara; to the dramatic annual wildebeest migration spreading dust far into the distance; to exceptionally crisp white coastlines flaunting palm-fringed beaches and bright blue waters. Safaris to Kenya are cherished experiences perfect for families, honeymooners, first time travelers and well versed explorers of the bush.
At Tsavo cats, we’re adventure seekers , explorers , dream chasers and travel experts who’ve collectively ventured to 100 plus destinations in the Magical Africa. We have unforgettable life-enriching trade experiences. We assure you of a magical experience in the hands of our professionals who deliver real expertise on flora , fauna and history of the region. We give you a chance to tailor make your dream holiday to your preference , taste and budget. Alongside our Safari consultants you will choose what to see, your duration of stay, level of accommodation you like (economy, comfort or luxury). Tsavo Cats Safaris is built on their passion for Africa and the technology that connects them with you and provides safaris to all destinations.



Tanzania Safaris

Safari happens almost everywhere you look in Tanzania. It’s been a way of life in East Africa’s second-largest country for decades. Few destinations in Africa can rival Tanzania's diversity of wildlife and landscapes. From the classic savannah destinations of the Serengeti, Tarangire and Ngorongoro Crater.



Kenya Safaris

Kenya is a safari destination that offers incredible natural beauty, soul-stirring cultural encounters, sensational game viewing and, of course, the thrills and spills of the Wildebeest Migration. No wonder it’s the birthplace of safari travel!
