13 Days Masai Mara & Amboseli Joining budget adventure


Enjoy our special package for three people where you get to enjoy the Masai mara, lake nakuru and amboseli national parks.

Tour
Itinerary

Here's a scope of the experience you will get

  • 01Nairobi Airport -Transfer to hotel

    We shall meet you at the airport upon arrival at the main airport and transfer you to hotel Booked on Bed and Breakfast. KAHAMA HOTEL NAIROBI

  • 02Nairobi -Lake Naivasha. Reserve.

    Start of your Safari!
    We shall pick you from the hotel at 7:30 am and start the tour with visit to Hell's gate National park with stop over at the view point 2700m, take some photos for some minutes then proceed and head to Naivasha where you visit the Hell's gate park for either walking or Biking inside the park. You will visit the Gorge and the fisher's tower. Afternoon have lunch then have optional of boat ride to see the Hippos and some birds at lake Naivasha. Then have dinner and overnight at TAPHE GUEST RESORT.

  • 03Lake Naivasha -Masai Mara..

    You will have breakfast, then depart and head to Masai Mara where you will arrive for lunch at either Lenchada Tourist camp or similar. Afternoon drive to the park for game drive till late evening. Dinner and overnight will be at the same camp. GOSHEN MARA CAMP/SIMILAR CAMP.

  • 04 Masai Mara -full day

    Spend the day in the park looking for the Big five and other plain games with lunch at Mara River as you watch hippos and Crocodiles. Late evening have optional of Masai Village visit till late evening. Dinner and overnight at the same camp. . GOSHEN MARA CAMP/SIMILAR CAMP

  • 05Masai Mara -full day

    Have another day to explore the park same with visit to Mara river for more wild animals including the wildebeest. Have the optional of walking tour with Masai people after the game drive. All meals at the same camp. GOSHEN MARA CAMP/SIMILAR CAMP.

  • 06 Masai Mara, Isebania boarder , Serengeti National Park.

    Early morning breakfast, then leave and head to the boarder of Kenya and Tanzania with game on the way. On arrival at the boarder you will buy the Tanzania visas, have lunch and chance of drivers as you proceed to Serengeti where you will arrive for dinner at Seronera campsite. SERONERA CAMPSITE.

  • 07 Serengeti full day

    You will spend the whole day in the park looking for the animals in this biggest park on Kenya and Tanzania parks. Here you will have chance to visit the Seronera River to see the wildebeest closing to Masai Mara Game reserve. All meals at the same campsite. Seronera campsite. SERONERA CAMPSITE.

  • 08Serengeti full day

    Spend another full day in the park looking for the big five and other plain games. You will have time for optional Masai Visit at the villages for the traditional dancing and culture, Balloon safaris before dinner and overnight at the same campsite. SERONERA CAMPSITE.

  • 09Serengeti -Ngorongoro National park.

    Early morning breakfast, then have sunrise game drive to the park as you leave for Ngorongoro you will arrive in the late afternoon for lunch, afternoon have game drive in the park before dinner and overnight at Simba campsite. SIMBA CAMSPSITE

  • 10 Ngorongoro Crater afternoon drive to Arusha

    Early morning breakfast, depart and head to the down of crater for more game drive 5-6 hours with lunch served at the crater. Then leave and head to Arusha where you arrive in the late evening for overnight at Green Mountain hotel booked on Bed and breakfast. GREEN MOUNTAIN HOTEL

  • 11Arusha -Namanga - Amboseli.

    You will be picked up from your hotel at 8:00 am for your shuttle bus transfer to Amboseli via Namanga boarder. No buying of another visa since you need only single visa entry. You will enter the park via Michenani gate few kms from Namanga proceed for game drive till late sundown. Overnight at NYATI CAMP

  • 12Amboseli -Nairobi.

    Early sunrise masai village and nature walk to see the Kilimanjaro, after proceed for the full breakfast before you depart for Nairobi where you arrive in the late afternoon for the hotel Booked on Bed and Breakfast. Overnight at KAHAMA HOTEL

  • 13Kahama Hotel - Airport

    You will be picked up from your hotel at 10:30 am the check out time, and then visit to the Giraffe center where you feed the Rothchild Giraffes, late by 1230hrs transfer you to the airport for the flight back home.

Includes

  • Transport on a safari van on Kenya and Jeep Tanzania side with an open roof for taking photography
  • Park entrance fees to ALL National parks and the Crater
  • Accommodation full board basis, Buffet meals, Breakfast, Lunch and dinner.

  • Mineral water while on the safari.
  • Pick up/drop from airport before and after the safari.
  • Hotel accommodation at Nairobi and Arusha booked on bed and breakfast.
  • Transfer from Arusha to Nairobi.
