4 days Ngorongoro, Serengeti and Lake Manyara safari

Enjoy our 4 Days tour of Climbimg Mt Kenya

This package goes for $520 per person which includes the following

Day Locations Accomodation Meals
1 Arusha - Lake Manyara. Panorama Safari Camp. Dinner
2 Lake Manyara - Serengeti National park. Seronera Campsite. Dinner
3 Serengeti -Ngorongoro crater. Simba Campsite. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
4 Ngorongoro - Arusha. Drive back to Arusha Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

Tour
Itinerary

Here's a scope of the experience you will get

  • 01 Arusha - Lake Manyara.

    Pick up from your hotel in Arusha, proceed to Lake Manyara for a day game drive in beautiful Manyara National Park. See buffalo, giraffe, zebra and many more species, all set against the stunning backdrop of the Great Rift Wall.

  • 02 Lake Manyara - Serengeti National park.

    Set out for the endless open plains and stunning skies of the Serengeti. A scenic road trip, with late evening game drive. Dinner and overnight at Seronera Campsite .

  • 03Serengeti -Ngorongoro crater

    Early cup of tea or coffee, head morning extended game drive, we later return to campsite have our brunch then proceed to Ngorongoro crater rim wit game viewing, where the night will be spent on the Crater Rim. Dinner and overnight Simba Campsite.

  • 04 Ngorongoro - Arusha.

    After an early breakfast, we proceed directly to the Crater floor for a full day crater tour. We'll have lunch at a picnic spot on the crater floor. We drive back to Arusha in the evening by 4pm time for your flight to Zanzibar.
