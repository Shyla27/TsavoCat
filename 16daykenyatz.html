16 Days Kenya Tanzania Joining Adventure


Enjoy our special package for three people where you get to enjoy the Masai mara, lake nakuru and amboseli national park.

Tour
Itinerary

Here's a scope of the experience you will get

  • 01Nairobi Airport -Transfer to hotel

    Arrival Date at Nairobi Airport.
    Meet and greet at airport upon arrival and transfer you to Hotel or Residency of your choice.

  • 02Nairobi -Lake Nakuru

    Start of your Safari!
    Depart after breakfast for 2 hrs drive with stop over at the view point escarpment 2700m. Proceed to Nakuru with optonal boat ride at Lake Naivasha, arrive Nakuru for hot lunch at Sopa Lodge. Afternoon game drives till late sundown. Overnight at Nakuru Sopa Lodge.

  • 03 Lake Nakuru- Masai Mara.

    Catch the sunset at Masai Mara

    After breakfast from the lodge depart for Masai Mara where you will arrive for hot luch at the camp, afternoon game drves till late sundown. Overnight stay at Loyk Mara Camp.

  • 04: Masai Mara full day

    After the breakfast, have the whole in the park looking for the big five and other plain games, you will explore the Greater mara till late sundown. Overnight at Loyk Mara Camp.

  • 05Masai Mara full day

    Early morning breakfast, have another day in the park looking for the big five and other plain games, all meals at the same camp. Overnight stay at Loyk Mara Camp

  • 06 Masai Mara- Isebania- North Serengeti

    Very early morning depart and head to Isebania boarder after the main breakfast, you will arrive at the boarder with picnic lunch from the camp in Mara, buy the Tanzania visas then proceed with the tanzania safari guide to the North Serengeti, this will be a long drive, please prepare for it. You will arrive at North Serengeti late evening for dinner and overnight at Serengeti Heritage Kogatende Camp

  • 07 North Serengeti -Full day

    You will spend the whole day in the park looking for the big five and seeing the migration. All meals at the same Kogatende Camp.

  • 08 North Serengeti Full day.

    Spend another day in the park, with game drives till late sundown. Overnight at Kogatende Camp.

  • 09 North Serengeti -Central Serengeti

    Early morning breakfast, depart with short game drive to the Central Serengeti where you arrive at Heritage seronera camp for the hot luch, afternoon game drive still late sundown. Overnight at Seronera Camp.

  • 10 Central Serengeti Full day

    Spend the full day in the park looking for the wild animals, all meals at the same camp.

  • 11 Central Serengeti- Ngorongoro Crater

    Depart after the morning breakfast, drive south to Ngorongoro where you arrive for the hot lunch at the camp. Afternoon game drive till late sundown. Overnight at Lion's Paw Camp

  • 12 Ngorongoro Crater- Tarangire.

    Early morning drive to the rim of crater, then aftern depart and head to Tarangire where you arrive for the hot lunch at Baobab Tent. Afternoon game drives till late sundown.

  • 13 Full day game drive Tarangire.

    Spend the day in the park looking for the big five and other plain games, all meals at the camp. Overnight at Baobab Tent.

  • 14 Tarangire -Namanga border- Amboseli

    Drive after the breakfast, head to Namanga where you will proceed to Amboseli arrive for the hot lunch at Tulia Camp. Afternoon game drives till sundown. Overnight at Tulia Camp

  • 15 Amboseli full day game drive.

    Spend the day in the park looking for the largest herds of Elephant and the view of Africa Highest Mt.Kilimanjaro. All meals at the same camp. Overnight at Tulia Camp.

  • 16 Amboseli National Park - Nairobi Airport

    Early morning sunrise game drives before the full breakfast, later depart and proceed to Nairobi where the tour end at the airport. End of Safaris
