Nairobi - Lake Nakuru


Enjoy our special package for three people where you get to enjoy the Masai mara, lake nakuru and amboseli national park.

Tour
Itinerary

Here's a scope of the experience you will get

  • 01Nairobi Airport -Transfer to hotel

    Arrival Date at Nairobi Airport Afternoon.
    Our safari guide will meet you upon arrival at the airport in the afternoon and transfer you to hotel Kahama Booked on Bed and Breakfast, relax at the hotel as you await for the next day safaris. Overnight stay at Kahama Hotel. Booked on Bed and Breakfast

  • 02Nairobi Kahama Hotel - Masai Mara Game Reserve.

    Start of your Safari!
    Our Driver-Guide will meet you at hotel in the morning at 7:30 am ready to join other tourits for the kenya safaris. departure for a scenic drive down into the Great Rift Valley, onwards to Masai Mara. You will be in a safari vehicle with an open roof. Stop briefly enroute at the Great Rift Valley viewpoint to enjoy the stunning views of what is the world's largest Valley, said to be even visible from space. Have hot lunch on the way to Masai Mara. The afternoon by 3:30 pm drive to the park for the game drives looking for the Big Five and other plain animals till sundown. Dinner and overnight at Mitimingi Eco camp or Goshen Mara Camp.

  • 03Masai Mara full day game drives.

    Enjoy a Full day in Masai Mara with Big Five and Migration.

    You will have a full day game drive [ one for the whole day] with a packed picnic lunch which will be served close to mara river as you watch hippos and crocodiles. Afternoon proceed for moore game drives to see the many wildebeest and the Big five among other plain games in the busy park, before you return in the evening for dinner and overnight at the lodge. Dinner and overnight at Mitimingi Eco camp or Goshen Mara Camp.

  • 04Masai Mara - Lake Nakuru.

    Leave Masai Mara for Nakuru.
    At the break of dawn you embark on your concluding game drive in the Mara followed by a relaxed breakfast and onward departure for Lake Nakuru with stop over at Lake Naivasha for optional boat ride experience. This very morning, you will option of Masai Village visit before you leave the camp. You will have hot lunch on the way to Nakuru, where you will arrive for dinner and overnight stay at the budget hotel Dinner and overnight stay at City Max Hotel.

  • 05 Lake Nakuru -Amboseli National Park.

    Leave Nakuru for Amboseli to see Kilimanjaro.
    After the early morning breakfast, depart and head for the game drive to see the Rhinos and Flamingoe, Pericans, Baboon and the Rothschild Giraffe. This park is known as the pink lake due to many type of birds species. You will then depart the park before the afternoon and head to Amboseli with lunch on the way, you will arrive Amboseli in the late evening time for dinner and overight at the camp. Dinner and overnight stay at Nyati Tented Camp

  • 06 Amboseli National Park Game Drives.

    Full day game drives with view of Kilimanjaro Mountain.
    After the early morning breakfast, have the whole day in the park lloking for the animals, you will have the best cnahce to see the Kilimanjaro at very early in the morning during the sunrise, The park is known for the largest heards of Elephant and the Masai Giraffes, you will have the packed lunch served at the observation hills. Also there is a small swamp inside the park where you will visit to see many hippos. Drive back to the camp for dinner and overnight stay. Dinner and overnight stay at Nyati Tented Camp.

  • 07 Amboseli - Nairobi

    Drive from Amboseli for Nairobi at hotel Booked on B/B.
    Depart the park with short game drives after the full breakfast, head back to Nairobi with lunch on the way, you will arrive back to Nairobi by latest 4:00 pm, The safari guide will drop you at the hotel in the city booked on bed and breakfast, then have a short city walk with a guide to see the street of Nairobi. You will pay dinner at your own either at the hotel or at the near restaurant.

  • 08 Hotel -Nairobi airport

    Pick up from your hotel on time after the breakfast, and transfer you to the airport for the flight back home.
